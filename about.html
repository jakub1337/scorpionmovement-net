ABOUT US

Scorpion Movement was established in December 2022 as an artistic collective.

The Scorpion Podcast premiered its first episode in August 2023, with the original hosts being Jakub, Jordan and FlyingCabala.

The aim of the show was originally to discuss philosophy, have conversations about the arts, culture and politics, and to foster a stronger community of the movement's members.

The movement’s solitary goal is to spread awareness of the importance of action primacy—the focus on habit and instinct—and to make fun of pointless dialectics.

We aim to do this in a largely comedic way, and we are a non-partisan organization with members and creators from all around the world.
